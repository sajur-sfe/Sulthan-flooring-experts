Who We Are

Our Story

Sulthan Flooring Experts was founded in 2003 by Master Craftsman Sajir Sulthan at his age of 16, whose passion for woodworking and meticulous attention to detail set the foundation for what would become one of Palakkad's most trusted flooring contract companies.

Started as a small startup in Mundur,Palakkad, Sajir built a reputation for quality that spread by word of mouth across the region. Each project was treated with the same care as his own home — and clients noticed.

Today, Sulthan Flooring Experts is leading into the path of success with the Founder Sajir, who combine decades of inherited wisdom with modern installation techniques and project management expertise. The tools have evolved; the commitment to excellence has not.
